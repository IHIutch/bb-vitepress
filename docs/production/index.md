# Production Access

Production access means your app works with real Medicare data for real enrollees. The Blue Button team reviews every app before granting production credentials. Here's what's involved.

## Checklist

Complete these steps to get production access:

1. **[Test in the sandbox](../guides/authentication-and-oauth.md)** — Build and test your app with synthetic data
2. **[Implement OAuth with PKCE](../guides/authentication-and-oauth.md)** — Full authentication flow working
3. **[Handle pagination](../guides/handling-pagination.md)** — Your app handles large EOB bundles
4. **[Build a consent flow](../guides/building-a-consent-flow.md)** — Users understand what data they're sharing
5. **[Write a privacy policy](./requirements.md#write-a-privacy-policy)** — Meets CMS requirements
6. **[Write terms of service](./requirements.md#write-terms-of-service)** — Consistent with your privacy policy
7. **[Optimize your app](../guides/going-to-production.md)** — Compression, efficient queries, error handling
8. **[Apply and demo](./application-review.md)** — Submit your application and demo for the Blue Button team

## Ready?

Start with the [detailed requirements](./requirements.md), or if you've already built your app, go straight to [applying for review](./application-review.md).
