## Key points

The full terms are the authoritative document, but here are the key things to know:

- **Data use** — You may only use Medicare data for the purposes described in your application
- **Privacy** — Your privacy policy must meet the requirements described in [Production Requirements](./requirements.md#write-a-privacy-policy)
- **Security** — You must protect enrollee data following industry best practices and applicable law
- **Consent** — Enrollees must actively consent to data sharing; you cannot access data without explicit authorization
- **Revocation** — Enrollees can revoke access at any time; your app must handle this
- **Changes** — Any changes to your privacy policy or terms of service require prior CMS approval
