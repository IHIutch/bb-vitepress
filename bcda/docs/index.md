# Beneficiary Claims Data API

BCDA gives eligible organizations bulk access to their Medicare enrollees' Parts A, B, and D claims data, including care received outside their network.

## Who is eligible

BCDA supports organizations participating in these [Alternative Payment Models](https://www.cms.gov/priorities/innovation/about/alternative-payment-models):

- [Medicare Shared Savings Program (SSP)](https://www.cms.gov/medicare/payment/fee-for-service-providers/shared-savings-program-ssp-acos)
- [Kidney Care Choices (KCC) Model](https://www.cms.gov/priorities/innovation/innovation-models/kidney-care-choices-kcc-model)
- [ACO Realizing Equity, Access, and Community Health (ACO REACH) Model](https://www.cms.gov/priorities/innovation/innovation-models/aco-reach)

If your organization participates in one of these models, BCDA can provide your attributed enrollees' claims data.
